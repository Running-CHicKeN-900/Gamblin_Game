Enhanced Guessing Game

How The Game Works
You start with 100 points. Each round you predict how many attempts it'll take you to guess a secret number, then wager points on it. The goal is to hit 10 jackpots in a row to win the game.
Outcomes each round:

Jackpot — guess it in exactly your predicted attempts → win 2x your wager
Near miss — within 1 attempt of your prediction → no loss, no gain
Loss — anything else → lose your wager
Go broke → game over, restart from scratch

Escalating difficulty:
Each jackpot increases the range by 100 (1-100 → 1-200 → 1-300 etc.)
Missing a jackpot resets the range back to 1-100
Streak multiplier increases jackpot rewards the further you go


Abilities:
Ability 1 — Jackpot Boost (max 4 levels)
Each skill point adds 5% to your jackpot reward. Passive, always active.
Ability 2 — Range Reduction (max 5 levels)
Each skill point reduces the guessing range by 5%. Passive, always active. Scales with the current range so stays useful even at 1-1000.
Ability 3 — Number Elimination (max 4 levels)
Eliminates 5 random numbers per skill point from the board at the start of each round, none of which can be the secret number.
Ability 4 — Double or Nothing (one time purchase, active)
Activated mid-round by typing "A4", but only within the first half of your predicted attempts. Doubles both your potential reward AND your potential loss for that round.
Ability 5 — Half Elimination (one time purchase, active)
Activated before the round starts by typing "5". Reveals which half of the range the secret number is NOT in, effectively halving your search space.
